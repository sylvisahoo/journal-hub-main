# requirement - Personal Journal and Diary Application

The Personal Journal application is a secure, private digital space where users can write, organize, and reflect on their daily thoughts. First and foremost, the app requires a secure account system so users can log in and ensure their personal writings remain completely private. Once inside the app, users need a straightforward way to create new diary entries. They should have access to a rich text editor, allowing them to format their writing with bold text, italics, and bulleted lists. Every entry needs to capture a title, the date, and the main content.

To keep things organized, users must be able to add customized tags or categories to their entries. As their journal grows, finding past memories becomes essential. The app needs a robust search feature that looks through the content and titles of all entries, alongside filters that let users narrow down their past writings by specific tags or date ranges. They should also be able to view their entries mapped out on a calendar.

While privacy is the default, users should have a toggle switch to make specific entries public, generating a secure link they can share with others. To keep users motivated and engaged, a visual dashboard should display their writing habits. This includes tracking how many consecutive days they've written (a writing streak), showing word counts, and displaying a calendar heatmap of their activity. Finally, users must be able to export all their entries to a document for safekeeping, and the entire application needs to be easy to type on and navigate using a mobile phone.
